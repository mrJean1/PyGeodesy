  • x_xyz - Scalar X component (meter), positive east or a local (XyzLocal, Xyz4Tuple, Enu, Enu4Tuple, Local9Tuple).
  • y - Scalar Y component (meter) for scalar x_xyz, positive north.
  • z - Scalar Z component for scalar x_xyz, normal positive up from the surface of the ellipsoid or sphere (meter).
