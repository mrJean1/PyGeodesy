Module ltpTuples

Named, Local Tangent Plane (LTP) tuples.

Local coordinate classes XyzLocal, Enu, Ned and Aer and local coordinate tuples Local9Tuple, Xyz4Tuple, Enu4Tuple, Ned4Tuple, Aer4Tuple and Footprint5Tuple.
